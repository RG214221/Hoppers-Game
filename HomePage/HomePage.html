Q. How do I unlock a level?

A. You need a certain amount of points in order to open higher levels:

  • For the intermediate level you need 500 points.
  • For the intermediate level you need 1000 points.

Q. How do I get points?

A. You recieve points for every card that you win according to the levels:

  • Beginning: 10 points
  • Intermediate: 20 points
  • Expert: 30 points

Q. What if I get stuck?!

A. You have a hint button! All the frogs that can be moved to another lily are highlighted in red.

Q.Can I lose points too?

A.Yes, you can!

  • If you lost a card you lose 100 points.
  • A hint costs you 20 points.

Pay Attention:

If you lose too many points, the level might become locked. Choose your moves wisely.
